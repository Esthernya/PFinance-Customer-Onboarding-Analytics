# 📊 PFinance Customer Onboarding Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the onboarding journey of 10,000 users on the PFinance mobile application to identify factors influencing successful customer activation. The analysis explores user acquisition, product experience, and customer support interactions to uncover opportunities for improving onboarding completion and business performance.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

PFinance observed that a significant proportion of new users were abandoning the onboarding process before becoming active customers.

The objective of this analysis was to identify where users dropped off, determine which acquisition channels and product experiences drove successful onboarding, and estimate the potential business impact of improving the onboarding completion rate.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Measure overall onboarding completion rate.
- Identify where users abandon onboarding.
- Compare onboarding success across acquisition channels.
- Compare Android and iPhone user performance.
- Evaluate onboarding success across app versions.
- Assess the relationship between customer support and onboarding.
- Examine customer satisfaction (CSAT) and onboarding completion.
- Estimate potential revenue generated from improving onboarding completion.

## 📈 Key Insights

- Overall onboarding completion rate was **63.87%**, with **6,387** users successfully completing onboarding.
- Facebook delivered the highest onboarding completion rate (**69.56%**), while Instagram attracted the largest number of users but recorded the lowest conversion (**50.42%**).
- Android users completed onboarding more successfully (**68.46%**) than iPhone users (**55.44%**).
- App Version **4.2.1** significantly underperformed compared to all other app versions.
- Customer satisfaction showed a strong positive relationship with onboarding completion, while support ticket creation showed little impact.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

- Increase marketing investment in acquisition channels with higher conversion quality.
- Investigate onboarding issues affecting iPhone users and App Version 4.2.1.
- Review onboarding flows for acquisition campaigns with low conversion.
- Improve onboarding experience to increase customer satisfaction.
- Increase onboarding completion to improve customer activation and revenue.
